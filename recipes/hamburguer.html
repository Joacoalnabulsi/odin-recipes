Homemade Pizza

Sure, you could buy hamburger buns at the grocery store — but where's the fun in that? It's easier than you think to make homemade hamburger buns, and they're way more delicious than the store-bought alternative.

  1. 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast (such as Fleischmann's ActiveDry Yeast®)
  2. 1 cup warm water (105 degrees F/41 degrees C)
  3. 2 large eggs, divided
  4. 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  5. 3 tablespoons white sugar
  6. 1 ¼ teaspoons salt
  7. 1 teaspoon olive oil
  8. 1 tablespoon milk
  9. 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, or as needed
